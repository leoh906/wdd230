Visual Hierarchy

BBC News

bbc.com/news

Visual Hierarchy is the concept of using changes in font sizes to show a hierarichal level of information. My research led me to observing the BBC News Homepage. In this snippet we see that a hierarchy is established. In the navigation bar the word news clearly defines the main point of the website. The links are significantly smaller to show that they might not be as important visually. In addition, there is a certain news article that has a bigger font size and a larger picture to establish a precedence for the more popular article.
